Our Services

Many services - To make you feel at home!

With all the services offered by Happy Hut, you will never miss home. Order delicious Filipino delicacies or create your own at home. You can also send money to your loved ones or give the money yourself by traveling back home. Happy Hut will do its best to let you feel that you are still home.

  • Party Food Orders

    We got you covered! Happy Hut is happy to bring its kitchen skills to your event. As a customer, you can request what meals you want us to serve. Share distinct flavours to your guests and show genuine Filipino hospitality at your event. You can book our catering services by calling 0383904212 / 0433917511.

  • Lunch Specials

    Our lunch specials changes daily and all of these meals are cooked during the day itself. All meals are cooked with only the freshest produce and ingredients imported from the Philippines. Have a taste of home by visiting Happy Hut! Worried that you will not be able to finish the meal? No sweat, takeaways are encouraged here.

  • Grocery

    Whip up your own homey Filipino dishes in the comfort of your own kitchen or munch on childhood favourites. Here at Happy Hut, everything is here from fresh produce, packaged ingredients, and delectable snacks. All goods are freshly imported from the Philippines!

  • Money Remittance

    Send love to your dearest ones here and abroad by using our money remittance service. All transactions are fast and secure so no worries. We can assure you that all cash delivered are safe and sound. To get updated of the exchange rate, please visit our Facebook page.
